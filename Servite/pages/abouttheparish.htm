About the Parish

This parish stretches from the River Thames in Chelsea to Old Brompton Road is part of the Roman Catholic Westminster Diocese.

Servite Friars
Our Lady of Dolours Servite church is under the pastoral care of the Servite Friars, since its beginning in 1864. For information on the Servite Friars, click here.   

The Servite Community in residence consists of the friars, Frs. Allan Satur, OSM and Chris O'Brien, OSM. There is no longer a Catholic Chaplain at the Chelsea and Westminster Hospital.

Parish Today
Fr Allan Satur OSM is the acting parish priest with help from Chris O'Brien, OSM. The parish today has evolved into a community of many races and nationalities. There are more than 30 parish groups that belong to the parish.
