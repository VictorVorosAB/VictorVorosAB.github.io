MASS TIMES

  There are special Mass times during Holy Week and Easter as detailed below.

HOLY WEEK & EASTER SERVICES 2019


  Saturday 13th April            
  6.30p.m.  Mass

  Palm Sunday of the Lord’s Passion 14th April 2019
  Masses as usual for Sunday:     8.30a.m, 10a.m, 11.15a.m, 12.15 a.m. & 7 p.m.
 10.00 a.m.   Mass (Blessing of Palms and Procession beginning in School yard at 9.30 a.m.)

  Monday 15th April            
  7.30p.m.   Lenten Penance Service
 
Tuesday 16th April     
12 noonChrism Mass in Westminster Cathedral
 
Maundy (Holy) Thursday 18th April
9 a.m. Morning Prayer of the Church
8 p.m. Mass of the Lord's Supper (followed by Adoration until midnight at the Altar of Repose)

Good Friday 19th April(Fast and Abstinence)
9 a.m. Morning Prayer of the Church
12 noon Stations of the Cross
3 p.m. Solemn Liturgy of the Passion with Holy Communion
8 p.m. Desolata and Meditation with Mary at the Foot of the Cross

Holy Saturday 20th April
9 a.m.Morning Prayer of the Church
10 a.m. - 11 a.m.Confessions
9 p.m.The Solemn Easter Vigil (with the Blessing of Easter Fire and the preparation of the Easter Candle in the Servite School yard).

Easter Sunday 21st April        
Masses:8.30 a.m., 10 a.m., 11.15 a.m. (Spanish) & 12.15 p.m. followed by blessing of Easter flowers and the crowning of Our Lady in Easter joy.

Please note: There will be no 7 p.m. Mass today.


During Easter Week Monday 22nd April until Friday 26th April,
there will be ONE Mass each weekday at 10 a.m.
No 6.30pm Mass

The Servite Friars: Frs. Pat, Chris, Allan, Paul with
Sr Clementina ESM, and the Parish Team & Council
would like to wish you and your loved ones a very
Happy & Peaceful Easter 2019.

We look forward to celebrating these Liturgies and the great Easter Feast with you
